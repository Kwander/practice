Buenos Aires

Argentina is a beautiful country, and Buenos Aires is the city I was born in, a city named after fresh air. It has a lot to offer from their rich culture to their yummy food, you will not be THAT disappointed maybe.

Things to do in Argentina



Asado

Asado is basically a barbecue and it's fitting because Argentine are a meat-eating people. They know the names of their cuts of meat, and it used to be that a lot of people owned their own cow. I recommend doing this because right now US money is worth a lot so buying a lot of meat is easier. Maybe try the morzilla it is like a sausage that is black that is apparently made out of blood. Although you do need to bring family or friends otherwise it is just eating meat.



El Coto De los juegos

I used to come here a lot as a kid and it was very fun. It's basically like Chuck E Cheese except it's a bit less crowded. They have a lot of arcade games and even air hockey. I even went there now as an adult visiting back. I think they may have been bought by another company it seems but this was a really good place.
